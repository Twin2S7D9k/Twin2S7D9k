# Pricing Packages

## Service Tiers

### Basic Tier
- **Price:** $10/month  
- **Included Services:**  
  - Access to basic features  
  - Email support  
  - Monthly performance reports  

### Standard Tier
- **Price:** $25/month  
- **Included Services:**  
  - All Basic Tier services  
  - Access to advanced features  
  - Priority email support  
  - Weekly performance reports  

### Premium Tier
- **Price:** $50/month  
- **Included Services:**  
  - All Standard Tier services  
  - Phone support  
  - Customized performance reports  
  - Early access to new features  

## How to Present These Packages to Potential Customers
1. **Understand Your Audience:** Tailor your pitch to meet the specific needs or pain points of the customer.
2. **Highlight Value:** Focus on what customers will gain from each tier, and how it can resolve their challenges.
3. **Use Visuals:** Create attractive infographics or charts that visually represent the differences between tiers.
4. **Offer Trials:** Consider offering a free trial or demo of the services included in higher tiers to give potential customers a taste of the benefits.
5. **Gather Testimonials:** Share success stories or testimonials from current customers who have benefited from higher-tier services.
6. **Follow Up:** After presenting, follow up with potential clients to answer questions and discuss options further.
